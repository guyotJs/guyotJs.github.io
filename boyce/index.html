Job Openings

Town Code Enforcer
The Town of Boyce is accepting letters of interest and resumes for the position of Code Compliance Inspector. Duties include but are not limited to educating and assuring compliance by residents with the Boyce Town Code, issuing warnings/citations as appropriate, preparing written correspondence, court appearances as required, and reporting to Town Council on monthly basis. The position offered requires 10 hours/week on a flexible schedule basis and pays $17.88/hour. Preferred qualifications include law/code enforcement experience, the ability to work with residents, town staff, and the general public. The applicant must possess strong verbal and written skills and be able to handle sensitive personal information in a discretionary manner. Applicant must pass a background check to be considered for this position. Please send a letter of interest and resume to Matt Hoff, Boyce Town Administrator USPS P.O. Box 209 Boyce Va. 22620 or email to boyceva@verizon.net. The position will remain open until filled.
