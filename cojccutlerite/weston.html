The old Cutlerite church in Clitherall Minnesota

    This church was operated by the True Church of Jesus Christ (Cutlerite) under the leadership of Clyde Fletcher, until the group became defunct in 1969. This building never belonged to the True Church of Jesus Christ (Cutlerite), and the land and building were owned by the original Church of Jesus Christ (Cutlerite).

    The Pearl of Great Price in the Cutlerite Church: The Pearl of Great Price as used in many of the larger Restorationist movements is rejected by the Cutlerite sect. This does not include the portion that is contained within the Inspired Version of the Bible. (The Book of Moses, and Joseph Smith Matthew) They reject The Book of Abraham, and reject the modern LDS idea of eternal progression.
